# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Novel Studio serves professional and semi-professional long-form fiction writers who maintain large local projects over months or years. They work in a desktop writing environment, often switching between chapters, story bible assets, timelines, prompts, agents, workflows, and AI suggestions.

## Product Purpose

Novel Studio is a local-first, project-based AI novel creation IDE. It organizes creative source material, chapters, history, prompts, agents, workflows, model settings, and AI assistance into an auditable workspace where the user remains the author and final decision maker.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, durable. The interface should feel like a professional editor's control room: dense, calm, keyboard-friendly, and built for long writing sessions rather than marketing impact or one-click generation.

## Anti-references

It must not look or behave like a chat wrapper, a prompt toy, a marketing landing page, a decorative card grid, or a single-model content generator. Avoid oversized hero layouts, ornamental gradients, visual clutter, and AI suggestions that appear to overwrite user authorship.

## Design Principles

- Writing remains the center of gravity; AI panels and inspectors support the chapter editor rather than competing with it.
- Every meaningful AI or plugin output stays inspectable and reversible before it becomes project truth.
- Local project files, schema validation, version history, and adapter boundaries are part of the user experience, not hidden implementation details.
- Product UI should favor density, clear state, and familiar IDE affordances over novelty.
- Progressive productization is acceptable only when gaps are explicitly tracked and not mislabeled as Product Ready.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for interactive text and controls. Preserve keyboard-first operation, visible focus states, reduced-motion compatibility, high-contrast dark-mode readability, and clear disabled/error states.
